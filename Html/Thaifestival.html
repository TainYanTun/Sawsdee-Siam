Thailand's Cultural Festivals

Discover the vibrant cultural festivals of Thailand, showcasing the country's rich heritage, spiritual traditions, and joyful celebrations throughout the year.

1

Water Festival (Songkran)

Thailand's New Year celebration with nationwide water fights.

2

Ghost Festival (Phi Ta Khon)

Colorful masked parade celebrating spirits and local folklore.

3

Lantern Festival (Yi Peng)

Thousands of lanterns illuminate the night sky in Chiang Mai.

4

Rocket Festival (Boon Bang Fai)

Homemade rockets launched to encourage rainfall for crops.

5

Buffalo Racing Festival (Wing Kwai)

Water buffalo races celebrating the end of rice planting season.

6

Lopburi Monkey Banquet

A feast prepared for thousands of macaque monkeys.

7

Vegetarian Festival

Intense spiritual ceremonies and strict vegetarian practices.

8

Wonderfruit Music and Arts Festival

Modern festival blending music, art, and sustainability.

9

Chinese New Year

Vibrant celebrations with dragon dances and red lanterns.

10

Candle Festival

Intricate wax sculptures during Buddhist Lent celebrations.

11

Loy Krathong (Floating Lantern Festival)

Beautiful lotus-shaped baskets float on water to honor the water goddess.

12

Elephant Round-up Festival

Spectacular elephant shows celebrating Thailand's relationship with these majestic animals.
